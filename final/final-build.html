Final Build Description...

Below, I will detail the triggers, rules, feedback, loops and modes.

Triggers - Hovering the mouse cursor over the image, clicking the image, the text, and the "X" (close) symbol.

Rules - When hovered over, the image will bounce up and down in place, and a "bounce/wobble" audio effect will play. The cursor will also change from the pointer to the finger state. Then, when clicked, a white title, description, and "X" (close) symbol will fade in over the image. A black overlay, (which has the dimensions of the image) at 0.7 opacity will also fade over on click, at the same time as the text. When this text is clicked, it will be replaced with additional text. When clicking the "X" (close) symbol, the text, symbol, and overlay will fade back out.

Feedback - The user will see the same things that are listed in Rules.

Loops - No loops in this microinteraction.

Modes - When the image is clicked, additional information appears (the title, description, and close symbol).
